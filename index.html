FOUNDER

ALI ABDUNOOR BUKHARI POOKKOTTUR

Usthad Ali Abdunnur Bukhari Pookkottur, a renowned Islamic scholar, orator, and educator from Kerala, India, is celebrated for his expertise in Islamic theology, jurisprudence, and the Arabic language. He has authored numerous insightful works on Islamic topics and delivered impactful lectures at various seminars and conferences, captivating audiences with his profound knowledge.

Actively involved in Islamic education, he has taught at several institutions in Kerala, nurturing students and promoting Islamic values. Respected for his dedication to scholarship and education, he continues to inspire and foster a deeper understanding of Islamic teachings.

ABOUT US

Manhajul Hidaya is an Islamic institution affiliated with Bukhari Islamic Dawah College. The institution aims to produce Islamic scholars who can contribute positively to society. To achieve this goal, Manhajul Hidaya offers language studies in English, Arabic, Urdu, and other languages.

By providing these language studies, the institution enables students to gain a deeper understanding of Islamic texts, communicate effectively with people from diverse backgrounds, and spread the message of Islam globally.
